About Thetechbrainer

I created Thetechbrainer.com to showcase my passion towards learning new technologies and share the knowledge with others. Most importantly I would like to keep things as layman as possible.


With everyday passing, I learnt importance of technology, networking & contributions to the OpenSource projects. With plenty of things to learn in today's world, I would like to keep track of what I learned & share with people who are interested to know more about technology topics.


So, We've decided to write one blog per week on specific topic and publish so that you can learn new things every week. If you have any specific topic you want us to cover in our blogs, feel free to contact us at Thetechbrainer@gmail.com
